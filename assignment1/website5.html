Sailing Courses at Sail Sand Point

Ready to embark on your own sailing adventure? Sail Sand Point has a course for everyone, whether you're just starting out or an experienced sailor. Check out these courses:

  • Intro to Sailing: Get to know the ropes and gain confidence on the water.
  • Intermediate Sailing: Sharpen your skills and learn some advanced techniques.
  • Advanced Sailing: Master the art of sailing and prep for racing or long-distance trips.
